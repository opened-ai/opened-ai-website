AI for Education Hackathon
July 21 - 28

Participate in-person (Delhi) or remotely

Calling all hackers! Apply your knowledge of CS, Data Science, Machine Learning, and NLP to build AI for education.

Work on any problem in education you're passionate about, e.g. personalization of content and delivery, intelligent assessments, instant quesiton-answering, clustering and classification of student learning styles, question intent-detection, etc.

Your outputs can be any of the following:

  • usable products (e.g. chatbots, web apps, mobile apps)
  • data sets (e.g. scrape public sources to collect data useful for future AI work in education)
  • tutorials / how-to articles for AI-education work you’ve previously done

Free resources we'll provide to help you get started:

  • Education-specific data sets (e.g. wikipedia, samlpe test questions)
  • Tutorials, pre-trained models, and code
  • Monetary prizes from our sponsors!

Why an open-source hackathon for Education AI?

The field of AI has and will continue to benefit greatly from open-source initiatives and the sharing of ideas, knowledge, and data. However, until now, research and applications of AI have pursued low-hanging fruit in highly capitalized industries, relegating “AI for education”, an admittedly difficult pursuit for both economic and technology reasons, to the realm of tech blog headlines and hype.

The mission of OpenEd.ai, and of this inaugural “AI for Education” hackathon, is to re-focus on what we can measurably achieve in this space and to inspire smart people to turn hype into results. We hope you'll join us.
